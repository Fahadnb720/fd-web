Meals of the Day

Breakfast

Idly with chudney

Idli is a savory, steamed rice cake that originated in South India and is a popular breakfast food in the region. It's made from a batter of fermented rice and black lentils, and is known for its soft, fluffy texture and mildly tangy taste. Idli is often served with sambar and coconut chutney.

Dosa chilly and coconut chudney

Dosa is a thin, savory crepe that originated in South India and is a staple of the region's cuisine.Dosa is famous for its simple ingredients and savory, slightly bitter flavor. It can be eaten as a snack, breakfast, or anytime you're in the mood for a delicious, savory meal! Originating in southern India, the crispy dosa has been around since the 5th century AD.

Conjee

Congee is a savory rice porridge that originated in China and is made by boiling rice in a large amount of water until the rice softens. The fermentation process not only gives kanjee its distinctive tangy flavour but also enhances its nutritional profile.

Lunch

Curd rice

Curd rice, also known as Dahi Chawal, is a healthy South Indian dish that's high in nutrients and has many health benefits.Curd rice is low in calories and high in protein, making it a good option for those looking to lose weight. The protein content helps in keeping you full for longer periods, reducing the urge to snack on unhealthy foods.

Rasam

Rasam is a classic example of traditional functional food with all its ingredients medicinally claimed for various ailments. Moreover, the processing in the formulation of rasam involves heating the spices in water and oil.

chicken curry with rice
